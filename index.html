How it works

How we serve our business clients.

PurpleFlow operates as a technology layer between our client businesses and their end customers on WhatsApp.

1

Client onboarding

A property agency signs up to PurpleFlow. We connect their WhatsApp Business number to our platform via the WhatsApp Business API and configure their conversation flow.

2

End users interact

The agency's customers message their WhatsApp number as normal. Our platform receives those messages and responds with a guided conversation on the client's behalf.

3

Leads delivered

Captured leads and escalations are surfaced to the client via their dashboard and direct WhatsApp notifications. The client's team follows up directly with their customers.

Platform data use

How we use WhatsApp platform data.

We operate as a technology provider on behalf of our business clients. Here is exactly how data from the WhatsApp Business Platform is used.

📤 What data we receive
When an end user messages our client's WhatsApp number, we receive the message content, the sender's phone number, and their display name. This is the only data we obtain from the platform.
🎯 How we use it
We use message content and phone numbers solely to operate the automated conversation flow and deliver responses on behalf of the client business. We do not use platform data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond delivering this service.
👥 How clients use it
Our client businesses use the captured enquiry data to follow up with their customers directly. They view lead details in their dashboard and contact customers by phone or WhatsApp to progress the enquiry.
🔒 Data handling
Platform data is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties. It is retained only as long as needed to deliver the service and is accessible only to the relevant client business and PurpleFlow staff for support purposes.
